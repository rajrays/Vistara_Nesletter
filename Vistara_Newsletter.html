2020 is off to a great start- we not only celebrated five incredible years of transforming air travel, but we also launched our fifth international destination, Kathmandu, Nepal. We will soon operate daily direct flights between Delhi and Kathmandu, offering the finest flying experience between the two countries.

This is just the beginning of an exciting year as we aim to rapidly expand our international footprint and double our international destinations in FY 21. We look forward to receiving the delivery of the Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner aircraft later this month that will be deployed on long-haul international routes.

And we will continue to expand our fleet to service our growing domestic and international destinations. Enhancing connectivity for our customers through strategic partnerships continues to be a priority. We recently signed a codeshare agreement with Lufthansa taking the total codeshare and interline agreements count to 32. This also marks an important step in broadening our customer base outside India and delighting inbound travelers with our world-class flying experience. I think all this stands us in good stead as we strive to strengthen our presence in the global marketplace.

Creating a positive impact in the communities we operate in has always formed an integral part of our culture. I am pleased that we celebrated our 5th anniversary milestone with our community partner, Smile Foundation and pledged to support the education and skill-building of 10 underprivileged young adults I would like to thank each one of you for your hard work and passion in making Vistara what it is today. We have achieved a lot together over the years. But what matters is what we do next and I hope that you share my excitement for the opportunities that lie ahead.

Sincerely,

Leslie

  • We signed a codeshare agreement with Lufthansa wherein Lufthansa will add its LH designator code to nearly 18 Vistara operated flights every day covering 10 Indian cities.

We will be supporting two ongoing programmes - Mission Education where we will be sponsoring the education of five young adults for five years, and Swabhiman to support five meritorious girl students for one year.

World-over, frequent flyer programs have emerged over the past three decades to be the biggest source of differentiation when it comes to customer experience in the aviation industry. Vistara, one of the newer entrants in this category is already creating ripples with the kind of service standards it is providing to travelers across the globe. Club Vistara, currently, the fastest rewarding frequent flyer program in the aviation industry is a lifestyle program that rewards its members with upgrades, free flights and lifestyle benefits. It aims to bring a seamless travel experience across various touchpoints of a customer journey.
